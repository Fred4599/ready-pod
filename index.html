Who is ReadyPod?

ReadyPod is a U.S.-based, veteran-led team focused on rapidly deployable modular housing. We support organizations that need housing solutions that can move quickly, perform in tough environments, and scale as demand changes.

Why ReadyPod?

ReadyPod units can be mobilized quickly, set up without a permanent foundation, and redeployed when project conditions evolve. This helps teams reduce response time, improve occupancy speed, and deliver dependable housing outcomes when timing matters most.

Who is ReadyPod?

ReadyPod is a rapid-deployment housing system designed for disaster response. Unlike FEMA’s reliance on hotels and rentals, ReadyPod delivers durable, independent homes that can be set up in half a day with no foundation required, providing immediate relief.

Why ReadyPod? Through our Strategic Housing Reserve, with 1,000 homes ready within days, ReadyPod ensures relief arrives in hours, not weeks. Sustainable and built to last, it’s more than housing - it’s a bridge to recovery.

Two homes fit into a single 40-foot shipping container, making it easy to ship and mobilize at a moment’s notice—by truck, rail, or ship.

In their container, BoxHouses are shielded from the elements, preventing exposure to moisture, mold, rust, and other hazards that can compromise health and structural integrity until it’s ready for use.

BoxHouses are foldable, steel-framed homes that do not require a foundation making them ideal for use on all kinds of soils or even parking lots.

Our Patented design makes a BoxHouse move-in ready in a matter of hours.

Constructed with high-quality, durable materials, BoxHouses are stronger and more rugged than traditional or manufactured housing—engineered to endure extreme environments.

ReadyPod units can operate completely off-grid with integrated solar and battery systems, providing reliable power anywhere it’s needed. Each unit can be equipped with rooftop solar panels and energy storage, allowing lights, appliances, HVAC, and communications to function independently of the grid. This self-sufficient setup ensures continuous operation during disasters or in remote locations, reducing reliance on external infrastructure and enabling true energy resilience.

When no longer needed, a BoxHouse can be folded back up and repurposed.

Rapid Deployment Capability

ReadyPod units are designed for speed. Having no need for a permanent foundation, our Duplex and Bungalow models can be delivered and operational within hours, allowing emergency response teams to provide immediate shelter to displaced families and critical personnel.

Industrial-Grade Durability & Strength

Built to withstand harsh environments, ReadyPod structures feature reinforced steel frames to ensure long-term resilience. Whether deployed in hurricane zones, wildfire aftermaths, or flood-prone regions, these units are engineered to perform under pressure and remain intact when traditional housing fails.

Complete Living Quarters

Each ReadyPod includes a private bathroom, full kitchen, sleeping areas, and climate control—providing comfort, safety, and privacy in even the most chaotic conditions. These turn-key living spaces eliminate the need for separate infrastructure, making them ideal for remote or compromised areas.

Scalable and Adaptable Footprint

ReadyPod’s two core models, the Duplex and the Bungalow, offer flexibility to suit a range of deployment needs. From single-family relief to temporary workforce lodging or mobile command centers, the units can be deployed individually or in clusters, depending on the scale and scope of the emergency.
