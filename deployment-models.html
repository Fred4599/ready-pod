Deployment Models

Configured to support a range of housing needs based on location, scale, and duration.

ReadyPod systems can be configured to support a range of housing needs based on location, scale, and duration.

Community Housing

Scale and efficiency for larger workforce and community needs.

Clusters of modular units arranged as housing villages with shared infrastructure. Ideal for larger workforce needs or community-based housing where scale and efficiency matter.

Individual Units

Flexible housing at the point of need.

Standalone units are placed directly at the point of need, including private land, job sites, or individual properties. Provides flexible housing without requiring a centralized development.

Scalable Project-Based Housing

Align housing capacity with project timelines.

Housing that can expand or contract as needs change over time. Allows organizations to align housing capacity with workforce levels or project timelines.
